Retirement Planning

Welcome to Investax, the ultimate destination for comprehensive retirement planning. With Rodel Claudio, our esteemed Financial Planner, leading the way, we are dedicated to empowering individuals like you to navigate the path towards a secure and fulfilling retirement. In addition to financial planning, we understand that tax and structure advice are crucial components of a well-rounded retirement strategy. That's why, when required, our Director Ershad Ullah gets involved in the conversation, bringing his expertise in tax and structure matters. Whether you're just starting to save or seeking strategies to maximize your existing investments, our expert resources and user-friendly tools are designed to support you at every step. With Rodel Claudio's guidance and Ershad Ullah's specialized tax knowledge, we provide personalized advice, a wealth of information, and interactive features to help you make informed decisions and create a retirement plan that aligns with your unique goals and aspirations.

How we
work with you

Step 1 Understanding your goals

At our first meeting we will discuss your current situation, what you are trying to achieve and why it’s important to you. We will then be able to discuss the range of financial options available to help you reach your goals.

Step 2 Design your financial strategy

Understanding your circumstances, goals and attitude to risk we will research and analyse different financial strategies to find the ones that are right for you. We then put together a presentation that details our recommendations.

Step 3 Presenting your financial strategy

Based on our discussions and research we will present you with a customised financial strategy which will set you on the path to where you want to be.

Step 4 Bringing your plans to life

We will work closely with you to implement your financial strategy step by step, and liaise with providers, technical experts and other professionals on your behalf.

Step 5 Staying on track with regular advice

We will stay in touch, ensuring your plan stays up to date as your life changes. Your strategies will be reviewed annually and you will benefit from our ongoing support and advice
