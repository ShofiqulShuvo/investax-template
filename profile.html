About Ershad

0286518000 ershad@investax.com.au

Ershad has been an accountant for more than 15 years. He is well known among his clients for delivering exceptional professionalism in key areas such as property investment and business consultation.

He is very passionate in helping his clients increase their personal wealth and grow their business by advising on various areas like tax minimisation, investment property, start-up business structures, restructure of business for future growth and exit strategies with estate planning. He has taken the Investax office to new heights with his trusted and passionate team of accountants.

He is also responsible for taking the Sydney practice into the modern era with the implementation of cloud-based technologies to better service his clients so they can focus on things that really matter to them. When you meet with Ershad you will not only get advice from a qualified and registered accountant but also an experienced property investor with multiple investment properties and a business owner who is striving for excellence.
