What makes us different

Why Investax

Welcome to Investax, your premier destination for Property and Business Tax Accounting services. We pride ourselves on being specialists in the field, providing reliable tax advice and exceptional accounting services.

Expertise in Diverse Investment
and Business Structures

Investax stands out from other accounting companies due to our team's extensive knowledge and experience in handling a wide range of investment and business structures like Trust, SMSF and Company. This expertise allows us to provide tailored solutions that effectively address the unique needs of our clients, ensuring their financial success.

Staying Ahead with Up-to-Date Tax Regulations
and Industry Trends

At Investax, we are committed to staying current with the constantly evolving tax regulations and industry trends. Our technical advisor teams consist of members of ‘NTAA’ and ‘Knowledge Shop’. Our leadership team consist of TPB, CPA, CA and IPA member.

Client-Centric Approach:
Building Trust and Reliability

Our client-centric approach sets us apart from other accounting companies. We prioritize building long-term relationships based on trust and reliability. We offer complimentary 5-10 minutes type correspondence throughout the year, ensuring that our clients' financial concerns are addressed without any concerns about costs.

Personalized Solutions for
Unique Circumstances and Goals

Investax understands that each client has unique circumstances, goals, and objectives. That's why we take the time to truly understand our clients' needs. Upon becoming a client, a dedicated Client Manager is assigned, along with a team of Senior Accountants devoted to your needs throughout the year. This personalized approach allows us to provide customized solutions and guidance that align with their long-term aspirations.

A One-Stop Destination for
Accounting, Finance, Financial Planning Services

With a comprehensive range of services, Investax serves as a one-stop destination for all tax and business needs including finance and financial planning. From lodging tax returns and BAS to managing IAS and STP, we offer a holistic suite of accounting services that cater to businesses of all types. This comprehensive approach ensures our clients can rely on us as their trusted partner in all aspects of their financial management.
